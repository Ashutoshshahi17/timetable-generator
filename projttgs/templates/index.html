Welcome to TTGS!, The primary objective of this project is to overcome the limitations of manual or traditional scheduling methods, which can be time-consuming, error-prone, and often fail to optimize resource utilization or minimize conflicts. By applying optimization algorithms, the project aims to improve the quality, efficiency, and fairness of the generated time tables.
